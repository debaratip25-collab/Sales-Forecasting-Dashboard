# Sales Forecasting & Demand Prediction Dashboard

📊 **Interactive Excel Dashboard** | Retail Analytics | Data Visualization

## Overview
A comprehensive Excel-based dashboard featuring real-time sales forecasting, demand prediction, and business intelligence across 4 regions and 5 product categories for 2023-2024.

## Features
✅ 120 realistic sales transactions
✅ 7 embedded Excel charts (line, bar, pie)
✅ 6 KPI cards with metrics
✅ Monthly forecasting with accuracy tracking
✅ Regional & category analysis
✅ Professional colour-coded design

## Dashboard Sheets
1. **Dashboard** — Interactive overview with charts & insights
2. **Sales Data** — Raw transaction data (120 rows × 21 columns)
3. **Monthly Summary** — 24-month aggregated trends
4. **Category & Region** — Breakdown by product & location
5. **KPI Metrics** — Business performance indicators

## How to Use
1. Download `Sales_Forecasting_Dashboard.xlsx`
2. Open in Excel/Google Sheets
3. Navigate to the **Dashboard** sheet
4. Charts update automatically (no macros needed)
5. Modify data in **Sales Data** sheet to test scenarios
